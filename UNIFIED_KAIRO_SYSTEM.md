## How They Work Together (Unified System)

The three projects are now designed to operate as one cohesive ecosystem with the **Business Idea Evolver** as the core autonomous engine:

- The **Kairo Central Brain** live dashboard is the command center that monitors and controls all agents.
- **Business Idea Evolver** continuously generates high-potential business ideas and revenue opportunities that feed directly into the 24/7 Revenue Swarm.
- **Lemma Health** agents can be spawned on-demand from the Central Brain for health-related experiments or user requests.
- All three systems share the same self-learning loops, reflection cycles, and revenue-generation principles.
- Data and improvements flow between them automatically via Notion, GitHub, and Box.

**Unified Principles**:

- Self-learning and continuous improvement
- Revenue generation as top priority
- Everything in one single source of truth (this file + repo + live dashboard)
